Analytics

Analytics is the systematic examination and interpretation of data to extract useful insights, identify patterns, and support decision making. Analytics can encompass a wide range of activities, from descriptive analytics, which describe what happened in the past, to predictive analytics, which use statistical models and machine learning algorithms to predict future events. Anyz

Visualization

Data visualization is the process of converting data into a visual representation, such as a chart, graph, or map, in order to make the information easier to understand and interpret. By visualizing data, patterns, trends, and insights can be more easily identified, and complex information can be communicated in a simple and intuitive manner. Viz

Machine Learning

Machine learning is a subfield of artificial intelligence that focuses on the development of algorithms and statistical models that allow computers to learn from data and improve their predictions over time. Machine learning algorithms use statistical techniques to enable computers to identify patterns and make predictions or decisions without being explicitly programmed to do so. ML

AI

AI (Artificial Intelligence) refers to the ability of machines to perform tasks that would normally require human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and language translation. It is achieved through machine learning algorithms and other technologies. AI
